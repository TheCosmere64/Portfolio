How to make a Pavlova

Ingredients

  • 3 egg whites
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 250g (9 oz.) caster sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 5 ml or 1 tsp vinegar
  • 5 ml or 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. cornstarch (optional)

============================================

Instructions

Step 1: Beat the egg whites and salt to a very stiff consistency.

Step 2: Add water and beat again before folding in caster sugar, vanilla and vinegar.

Step 3: Beat until the mixture holds its shape and stands in sharp peaks.

Step 4: Pour the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

Step 5: Slow-bake the mixture at 150°C (300°F) to dry all the moisture and create the meringue, approximately 45 minutes.

This leaves the outside of the pavlova a crisp crunchy shell, while the interior remains soft and moist.

Tips and Tricks

A trick to use (although untraditional) is to turn the pavlova upside down before decorating with cream and fruit.

This is because the bottom is less crispy than the top after cooking and unless you seve it immediately after decorating the "top" absorbs moisture from the cream.

Another tip is to leave the pavlova in the oven after turning off the heat - this helps to prevent the middle of the pavlova from collapsing (although if it does collapse, generous application of cream can hide any mistakes!)
